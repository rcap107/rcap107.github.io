Embeddings

Embedding models describe methods for transforming “regular data” into modified representations that somehow “encode” some property of the data. These representations are implemented as “vectors” and have a number of nice properties that can be exploited for a number of applications. I will explore these concepts in a series of posts here. As EmbDI is based on embeddings, these series will be strongly connnected to each other.
